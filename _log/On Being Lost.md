While in reality I was on survival mode for most of that time. Oscillating barely above the poverty line. Few months or weeks away from being broke at all times.

I've quit before I would get fired anyway. I carry the scars from few of these boom and bust cycles that shake the street with greed. I was born on the other side of the river, under a stone in a falling communist regime. I saw first KFC opening in my country. I could not afford the bucket then.

And I'm currently honestly lost in all of this crap that goes around.
