_During the fourth and fifth world wars, the tanks_

_Will still obey, still seem to serve their humans..._

 _The sixth war they will serve more sullenly--_

_And suddenly will know their day has come._

_The birthday of the Prince of all the tanks._

_And then will humans all be jitterbugs,_

_Migrate like locusts from their dance-hall doors,_

_And sing with insect-voices metal shrill:_

_"Our god is born!" and roll to him like grapes_

_Till all their frenzy begs his metal treads:_

_"Love us to death, love us to death" the day_

Creation's final goal, Prince Tank, is born.

- Peter Viereck
