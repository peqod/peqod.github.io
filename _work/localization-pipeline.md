*Agency work — client assets are not shown here. This is the process story, which is the interesting part anyway.*

Seven years directing visual output for **Canon, Ferrero Rocher, and CAT** across EMEA and global markets. The job sounds like design; the problem is logistics: multiple concurrent campaigns, each shipping in **40+ languages**, every version pixel-perfect against brand guidelines.

## The bottleneck

Manual multi-language content delivery doesn't scale. Every locale is a re-layout, every re-layout is a review cycle, and review cycles multiply by language count.

## The fix

Designed and shipped a **data-driven asset pipeline** that standardised multi-language delivery: content and layout separated, locale variants generated from data, review reduced to exceptions instead of everything.

Result: **5× faster turnaround** with no quality loss — measured across campaigns that previously took the full manual cycle.
