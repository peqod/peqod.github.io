## Lightcraft (2007-2010)

VFX and motion graphics for TV commercials and film festival content, including **daily broadcast spots for Gazeta Wyborcza**. Brief to final animation: concept, boards, animation, compositing, delivery. 
I've unfortunately lost the period proper reels. 
What remain is a handful of stills and many stories.

![[Gazeta_Wyborcza 1.jpg]]
GW was a tight project for the time: brief, quick ideation, block-out, animation and polish needed to happen twice or sometimes thrice a week without fail. Mind you, it all needed to go to beta tapes. Good templates and hard scope freezing rules made this possible and enjoyable though.

## Agora Publishing: SVG Studio (2010–2011)

Ran design and production for a **360° in-house studio** at one of Poland's largest media companies: cinema, TV, print, digital. Managed cinema DPX delivery and DVD authoring pipelines.

I cannot track reel from that time too, despite backups! And that's a loss. But I have some spare clips from the times.  First goes tuba.fm - I was always a proponent of organic, hand-made animations. This spot was made on exactly this premise. Playful, real and fun. I've designed and animated all the assets in after effects in one day or so.
